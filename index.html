About

Built by someone who speaks finance

The Credit Card Compass was founded by Erin — a sales professional turned entrepreneur who has spent years immersed in the world of personal finance. Her husband owns and operates a wealth management firm, which means the nuances of financial products have always been part of the conversation at home.

That background gave her a clear-eyed view of what most people get wrong about credit cards: they pick based on marketing, not math. The Credit Card Compass exists to fix that — with honest, independent analysis of what each card actually costs and who it's actually right for.
