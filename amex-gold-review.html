Overview

The American Express Gold Card occupies a sweet spot in the credit card market: mid-tier annual fee, premium rewards rates on the categories most people actually spend in. While the Amex Platinum targets jet-setters and the Sapphire Reserve targets frequent travelers, the Gold targets people who eat out regularly and do their own grocery shopping.

The 4x earn rate on dining is the best available on any card — beating the Chase Sapphire Reserve (3x) and Capital One Venture X (2x) by a significant margin. At US supermarkets, 4x applies to the first $25,000 in purchases per year — enough to cover virtually all households.

The $250 annual fee sounds steep for a mid-tier card, but the $120 dining credit ($10/month at eligible restaurants) and $120 Uber Cash ($10/month) together offset $240 of the fee — leaving an effective cost of just $10 per year for anyone who uses them.

Welcome Bonus

New cardholders earn 60,000 Membership Rewards points after spending $6,000 in the first 6 months. At 1.8¢ per point, that's worth approximately $1,080 in travel — more than four times the annual fee in year one.

The $6,000 spend requirement over 6 months works out to $1,000 per month — very achievable if you're putting dining and grocery purchases on the card regularly.

Key Benefits and Perks

🍽️ 4x on Dining Worldwide
4 points per $1 at restaurants worldwide
This applies to restaurants, cafes, bars, lounges, fast food, and food delivery services worldwide. At $500/month in dining (a realistic figure for someone who eats out regularly), you'd earn 24,000 points per year — worth $432+ in travel.
🛒 4x at US Supermarkets
4 points per $1 at US supermarkets (up to $25,000/year)
The $25,000 annual cap is enough to cover virtually all households. Note this applies to traditional supermarkets — warehouse clubs like Costco and Sam's Club typically don't qualify, and neither do superstores like Walmart or Target.
🍕 $120 Annual Dining Credit
$10/month at eligible partner restaurants
Get up to $10 back each month when you use your Gold Card at eligible dining partners including Grubhub, Seamless, The Cheesecake Factory, Goldbelly, Wine.com, and Milk Bar. You must enroll each year to activate this benefit.
🚗 $120 Uber Cash
$10/month in Uber Cash for rides and Uber Eats
Get $10 in Uber Cash each month, automatically loaded to your Uber account when you link your Gold Card. Works for both Uber rides and Uber Eats delivery. If you use Uber even once a month, this is essentially free money.

Who should get this card: Anyone who regularly eats at restaurants, orders delivery, uses Uber, and does their own grocery shopping. This is the best everyday rewards card for urban and suburban households.

Who should skip this card: Frequent travelers who want lounge access and travel credits — the Chase Sapphire Reserve or Venture X are better fits. People who primarily spend on gas or other non-dining categories.
