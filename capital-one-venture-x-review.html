Overview

The Capital One Venture X launched in 2021 and immediately disrupted the premium travel card market. At $395 per year, it sits comfortably below the Chase Sapphire Reserve ($550) and Amex Platinum ($695), yet offers a comparable benefit stack — including a $300 travel credit, lounge access, and premium travel protections.

The card earns 10x miles on hotels and rental cars booked through Capital One Travel, 5x on flights booked through Capital One Travel, and an industry-leading 2x miles on every other purchase. That flat 2x rate means you're always earning at a strong clip regardless of spending category.

Perhaps the most underrated feature: 10,000 anniversary bonus miles every year starting at year one. Worth at least $100 in travel, this alone covers more than 25% of the annual fee before you use a single other benefit.

Welcome Bonus

New cardholders earn 75,000 miles after spending $4,000 in the first 3 months. At 1¢ per mile minimum, that's $750 in travel — and potentially significantly more when transferred to airline partners like Air Canada, Turkish Airlines, or Avianca.

The $4,000 minimum spend over 3 months works out to roughly $1,333 per month — very achievable for most cardholders.

Key Benefits and Perks

💳 $300 Annual Travel Credit
$300 credit through Capital One Travel portal
Unlike the Amex Platinum's airline fee credit (which only covers incidentals), the Venture X travel credit covers actual travel purchases — flights, hotels, rental cars — made through Capital One Travel. Easy to use, automatically applied. This drops the effective annual fee from $395 to $95.
🎂 10,000 Anniversary Miles
Worth $100+ in travel every year
Every year on your account anniversary, you receive 10,000 bonus miles — worth at least $100 in travel. Combined with the $300 credit, this means the card effectively costs -$5 per year for anyone who uses both benefits. This is one of the most generous anniversary bonuses of any premium card.
🛋️ Priority Pass + Capital One Lounges
1,300+ Priority Pass lounges + Capital One's own lounges
Venture X cardholders get full Priority Pass membership plus access to Capital One's own lounges — currently at Dallas Fort Worth, Denver, Las Vegas, and Washington Dulles, with more opening. Capital One Lounges are widely regarded as excellent — spacious, uncrowded, with good food and drink options.
🌐 2x Miles on Everything
Flat 2x on all purchases outside the travel portal
No other premium travel card earns 2x on all purchases. This makes the Venture X a legitimate everyday card, not just a travel card. On $3,000/month in spending, you'd earn 72,000 miles per year from everyday purchases alone — worth $720+.
🛡️ Travel Protections
Trip cancellation, delay, and lost baggage coverage
The Venture X includes trip cancellation and interruption insurance, trip delay reimbursement, lost luggage reimbursement, and primary rental car insurance — comparable to the Chase Sapphire Reserve's coverage.
